Crop Consulting

Crop Consulting is a job where the hired person scouts fields and then recommends to farmers what is needed. For example, when a crop is dying over a period of time, we can help recommend what to do to save the crops and help the farmer's plants grow healthier.



Soil Sampling

Soil Sampling is a job where the worker takes soil from different parts of a field and sends it to a lab to determine what is needed to make the soil more healthy. We offer random, grid, and zone soil sampling and can send variable rate files to any controller on the market. We are dedicated to pleasing our customers and not cutting any corners when soil sampling.



Lime Spreading

We take care of hauling and spreading or, if you want to haul it yourself, we will price just the spreading. We can do Straight rate or VRA. Lime Spreading has been a very successful part of our buisness and we hope that all of our customers are pleased with our services.
