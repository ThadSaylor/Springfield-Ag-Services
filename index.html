We are a locally owned family business in Brownsville, Tennesee. This business consists of hard workers willing to do whatever it takes to satisfy our customers. Our objective is to help farmers improve productions while protecting the environment.


We meet with farmers to discuss their plans for the upcoming crop year. We go over fertility issues, what crops will be planted where, and the varieties that would be best for each field. After crops are planted, we scout their fields once per week and write recommendations for insect control, weed management, growth regulators, water control, and fertility needs.






We offer random, grid, and zone soil sampling. We can also send variable rate files to any controller on the market. We are dedicated to pleasing our customers and not cutting any corners when soil sampling.




We take care of hauling and spreading or, if you want to haul it yourself, we will price just the spreading. We can do straight rate or VRA. We will help you select the best lime source for your situation.
